Coordinated Campaigns and Information Integrity During the 2023 Israel-Hamas War on Twitter

This study uncovers how coordinated campaigns shaped the information environment on Twitter during the 2023 Israel-Hamas War, analyzing 4.5 million tweets across 11 coordinated groups using multimodal methods.
